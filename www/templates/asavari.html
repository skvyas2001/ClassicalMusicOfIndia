About the Thaat Asavari

Raags in Thaat Asavari are mostly characterized by Komal Ga Komal Dha and Komal Ni. These Raags theoretically sound most beautiful between late night to morning. The most popular Raags in Thaat Asavari are Raag Jaunpuri, Raag Asavari and Raag Darbari-Kandra.
