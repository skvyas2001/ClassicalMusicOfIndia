About Raag Basant

Raag Basant has both Tivra and Suddha Ma. Raag Basants Vadi Sur is Sa and Samvadi Sur is Ma. Komal Re and Komal Dha are used in Basant. In the Aaroha, Re and Pa are absent. The most suitable time for Raag Basant is 4am - 7am.

Basant is not heavily used in Semi-Classical compositions and therefore there are not many Popular Bollywood Songs in Raag Basant. One is Basant Hai Aaya Rangeela by Asha Bhonsle and Mahendra Kapoor from Stree(1961).
