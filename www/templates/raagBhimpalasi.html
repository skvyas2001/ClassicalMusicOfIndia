About Raag Bhimpalasi

Raag Bhimpalasi's Vadi Sur is Ma and Samvadi Sur is Sa. Re and Da are absent in the Aaroha of Bhimpalasi. Bhimpalasi has Komal Ga and Komal Re. The most suitable time for Bhimpalasi is 4pm to 7pm.

Popular Bollywood Songs in Raag Bhimpalasi include Kuch Dil Ne Kaha by Lata Mangeshkar from Anupama(1966), Maine Chand Aur Sitaron Ki by Mohd Rafi from Chandrakantha(1956), and Khilte Gul Yahan by Kishore Kumar frmo Sharmilee(1971).
