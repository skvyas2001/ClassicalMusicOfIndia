About the Thaat Bhairavi

Thaat Bhairavi is primarily characterized by having all 4 Komal Swars(Re, Ga, Da, and Ni). Raags in Thaat Bhairavi are meant for the Early Morning and Compositions are mostly devotional. The most popular Raags are Raag Malkauns and Raag Bhairavi.
