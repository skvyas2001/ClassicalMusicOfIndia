About the Thaat Bhairav

Thaat Bhairav is also considered a Basic Thaat. The most prominent feature of Raags in Thaat Bhairav are Komal Dha and Komal Re. These Raags are suitable for Early Morning and the Compositions are typically Devotional. The most popular Raags are Raag Bhairav and Raag Ramkali.
