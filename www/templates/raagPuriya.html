About Raag Puriya

Raag Puriya has Komal Re and Tivra Ma. Pa is completely absent in Puriya. Puriya's Vadi Sur is Ga and Samvadi Sur is Ni. The most suitable time for Puriya is 10pm - 1am.

Raag Puriya is stricly for classical compositions due to the nature of the Raag. Therefore there are no Popular Bollywood Songs based off of Raag Puriya.
