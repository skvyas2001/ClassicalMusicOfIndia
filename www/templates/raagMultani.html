About Raag Multani

Raag Multani's Vadi Sur is Pa and Samvadi Sur is Sa. Multani has Komal Re Komal Ga Komal Dha and Tivra Ma. Re and Da are absent in the Aaroha. The most suitable time for Multani is the 4th Part of the Day.

Raag Multani is rarely used properly in Semi-Classical Compositions. There is only one pure example of Multani which is Lag Gayi Akhiyan by Lata Mangeshkar from Jeevan Jyoti(1953).
