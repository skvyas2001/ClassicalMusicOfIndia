About Raag Desh

Raag Desh's Vadi Sur is Re and Samvadi Sur is Pa. Raag Desh does not have Ga and Ni in Avroha. Both Suddha Ni and Komal Ni are present in Desh, the most suitable time to sing Raag Desh is 10pm - 1am.

Popular Bollywood Songs in Raag Desh include Taqdeer Ka Fasana by Mohd Rafi and Lata Mangeshkar from Sehraa(1963) and Aap Ko Pyar Chupane Ki Buri Adat Hain by Mohd Rafi and Asha Bhonsle from Neela Akash(1965). The original Indian National Anthem written by Rabindranath Tagore, Vande Mataram, is Purely Raag Desh.
