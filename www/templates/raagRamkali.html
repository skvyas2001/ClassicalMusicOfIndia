About Raag Ramkali

Raag Ramkali has Komal Re Komal Dha and both Tivra and Suddha Ma. Ramkali's Vadi Sur is Pa and Samvadi Sur is Sa. The most suitable time for Raag Ramkali is 4am - 7am.

Raag Ramkali is rarely used properly in Semi-Classical Compositions. Therefore there are no Popular Bollywood Songs based off of Raag Ramkali. Above is a Drut Composition performed by Pt. Jasraj in Raag Ramkali.
