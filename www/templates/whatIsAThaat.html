About the Thaat System

Thaat is the basic structure for all Indian Classical Music. A Thaat is comprised of all 7 basic Swaras that are collected to make a Raag. Each Thaat differs in the the use of Komal(Flat) or Tivra(Sharp) Swaras, and the nature of singing in the Thaat.

Pandit Venkatmakhi theorized that is it possible to have 72 Thaat by counting the various combinations of Komal and Tivra swaras. Of these 72 Thaats, Pt. Vishnu Narayan Bhatkande chose 10 of the most inclusive Thaats and created a basic structure of them. Those Thaats are Asavari, Bhairav, Bhairavi, Bilawal, Kafi, Kalyan, Khamaj, Marwa, Purvi, Todi.
