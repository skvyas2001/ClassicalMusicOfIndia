About the Thaat Kafi

Thaat Kafi is one of the oldest Thaats in existence. It was a very popular ancient Thaat most suitable for the evening. The prominent characteristics are Komal Ga and Komal Ni. The most popular Raags are Raag Bageshri and Raag Bhimpalasi.
